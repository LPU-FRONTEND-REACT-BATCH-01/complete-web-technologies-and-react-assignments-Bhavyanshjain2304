Steps to Cook Rice

  1. Rinse 1 cup of rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Optional: Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes.
  3. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil. Add salt.
  4. Add rice, stir once, reduce heat.
  5. Cover and cook for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Fluff and let sit for 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot.
